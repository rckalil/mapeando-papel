Qualities and Exports Correlation

Exports vs. Qualities Scores

About the visualization:

The visualization aims to illustrate the correlation between increasing exports and the quality of exported coffee. It shows that many countries, including Brazil, received lower quality scores as their exports increased, indicating a decline in quality with rising demand. In contrast, other countries, such as Colombia, managed to increase their quality scores alongside export growth, further justifying their growth through improved quality.

About the data:

The exports data was generated from United Stares Department of Agriculture and is available at Kaggle.

The quality data was scraped from Coffee Quality Institute database and is available at Kaggle.

Through exploratory data analysis the correlation between quality ratings and the quantity of coffee sacks exported by each country was calculated.
