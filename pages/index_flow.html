About the visualization:

This page aims to visualize the travel of coffee, in kilos, from Brazil to the world during the last years.

Thus, a coffee lover like us can find out the greater consumers and which countries have a similar consumption! Even further, we can find periods of time that changed the coffee market.

The data was collected from the Comex Stat System and the open data from SECEX - Secretaria do Comércio Exterior. The visualization was developed using D3.js.

About the data:

The dataset is available at Gov.br. The data was grouped so that the values of traffic corresponding to the same time, destiny and coffee wrapper, are summed up.
